Luka Doncic

Luka Doncic is a Slovenian basketball player that plays for the Dallas Mavericks. He was an 3rd overall pick in the 2018 NBA draft by the Atlanta Hawks but got traded to the Dallas Mavericks. During his first season as an NBA player, he was named Rookie of the Year and was part of the all-rookie first team. After that, he was named to five all-star teams by 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024. In the 2022 season, he helped the Mavericks to their first Western conference finals, after years of not being in it. Up until now, he is one of the rising stars in the NBA. This season, he's averaging 34.7 points per game, 9 rebounds per game and 8 assists per game.
